LOGO SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
=======================

To add your Brel Cafe logo to the application:

1. Copy your "brellogo.png" file to this folder:
   frontend/assets/images/brellogo.png

2. Make sure the file is named exactly: brellogo.png

3. The logo will automatically appear in:
   - Header of customer menu
   - Footer of customer menu  
   - Admin panel header
   - Login page

LOGO REQUIREMENTS:
- Format: PNG (recommended for transparency)
- Size: Any size (will be auto-resized)
- Background: Transparent preferred
- Colors: Works with light and dark themes

Your beautiful Arabic "برێل کافێ" logo will be displayed throughout the app!
